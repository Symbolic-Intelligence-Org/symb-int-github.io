Most AI systems in production can't tell you why they did what they did. The model gave a number, the agent called a tool, something got written to a database. Tracing it back is hard or impossible. That's fine until a regulator shows up, a customer pushes back, or another system downstream chokes on bad data.
We help with that. Mostly we get pulled in when a team has shipped something agentic and now needs to make it defensible.

What we do:

- look at your agent setup and tell you where the holes are
- bolt provenance and deterministic checks onto pipelines you've already built
- integrate our software where it fits
- build out evaluation and audit tooling for systems with more than one agent in the loop

We're a good fit if you're in a regulated space, or if you're running multi-agent systems and the cracks are starting to show. Less useful if you just want a chatbot.
If you've got something in production you can't fully reconstruct, email us.
